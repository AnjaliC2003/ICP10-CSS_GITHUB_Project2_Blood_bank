Blood Donation Doubts

Donor Eligibility

  • What is the minimum age to donate blood?

    • You must be at least 18 years old to donate blood.

  • What is the minimum weight to donate blood?

    • You must weigh at least 50 kg to donate blood.

  • Can I donate blood if I have a medical condition?

    • It depends on the medical condition. Please contact us to determine your eligibility.
